# AUI Todo App 

Its a simple Notes App made with [AUI framework](https://github.com/aui-framework/aui).

Unlimited Note space to track any amount of tasks you want. With a simple UI that makes it easier and more conviniet than a sheet of paper and less clutered than a notes app on your phone.
